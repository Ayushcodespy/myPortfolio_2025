About me

I'm Ayush Patel — a self-motivated B.Tech student and passionate full-stack developer. My journey into the digital world began with photography, where I discovered the joy of creating visuals from scratch. That creative spark led me into software development, where I now channel my skills into designing meaningful and user-friendly applications.

I specialize in building modern web and mobile solutions using Django, Flask, and Flutter. I’ve built several impactful platforms like ARYA-Z-TECH, and I constantly strive to merge creativity with functionality. My goal is to deliver clean, scalable, and intuitive digital experiences while always staying curious and eager to learn.
